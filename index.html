— Who We Are

We believe every woman
deserves a seat at the table.

Queens Of Change Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to unlocking the potential of women and girls through access to education, mentorship, and professional opportunities.

Founded in 2018, we've partnered with communities across the country to deliver programs that address real barriers — from skill gaps and financial exclusion to confidence and representation.
